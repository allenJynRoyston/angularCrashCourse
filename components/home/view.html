It's A Time Saver

Using Angular can save you a ton of time. For as awesome as jQuery is, having to depend on event binders, append/prepends, and traversing the DOM to manipulate your page can be quite a headache. Using Angular you can eliminate quite a bit of code.

There's a Ton of Plugins

There's also an extensive library of modules you can use to extend the functionality of your web app that range from anywhere from google maps, to web cam control, to speech integration, to youtube videos. And that's just a small sample. You can find pretty much anything you could possibly need here.

It's The Easiest to Learn

There are several other frameworks that can do what Angular does (EmberJS, Handlebars, Backbone, etc), but Angular (in my opinion) is probably the easily to learn, in part due to the large community of developers and supporters. 99% of every question I have ever had somebody else has already asked and a large majority of them have been answered by just a simple Google search.
